My Background

My journey into tech started with a curiosity about how data can be used to solve real-world problems. This led me to pursue the ALX Data Science program, where I built a solid foundation in Python, SQL, data analysis, and machine learning through hands-on, project-based learning.

Along the way, I discovered a growing interest in how users interact with technology, which pushed me to expand into web development. I’m currently training at Umuzi and BBD, where I’m gaining practical experience in building responsive and user-focused web applications.

In addition to my technical journey, I’ve taken on leadership roles as a Student Group Discussion (SGD) lead and deputy Salesforce squad lead. These experiences have strengthened my communication, teamwork, and mentorship skills, and shaped how I approach problem-solving—collaboratively and with impact.

Overall, my background reflects a blend of data, development, and leadership, all driven by a desire to continuously learn and create meaningful solutions.
